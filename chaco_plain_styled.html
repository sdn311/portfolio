Chaco Plain Retroarc Foreland Basin of South America

A textbook example of a Distributive Fluvial System

Dr. Gary Weissmann, a professor of hydrology at the University of New Mexico, has studied fluvial systems for a majority of the past two decades. As an undergraduate student under his advisement, I have been conducting research examining the Chaco Plain in South America to understand the geomorphic, fluvial, and sedimentary characteristics of this region.
